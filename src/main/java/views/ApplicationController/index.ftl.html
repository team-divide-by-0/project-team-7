B A T T L E S H I P

  • Set-up:

  • Opponent is the top board
  • You are bottom board
  • You can place any 3 ships on your board
  • Your ship can be placed horizontal or vertical

  • Playing the Game:

  • Click on squares on the opponents board to shoot
  • A hit will appear red and a miss will appear white
  • Hit
  • Miss
  • Hit all your opponent's ships to win the Game!

  • Cool Features in the Game:

    • Captain's Quarters

  • Try to hit the Captain's Quarters! If the captains quarters is hit, then the ship is immediately sunk!
  • Beware! The captain's quarters for battleships and destroyers is armored. It takes 2 attacks on the same square for it to be hit
  • This means that the first attack always counts as a miss
  • Each ship has a unique captain's quarters. The red box indicates where the captains quarters is within each ship
  • Minesweeper
  • Destroyer
  • Battleship

  • Submarine

    • Sonar Pulse

  • The sonar pulse allows you to reveal a portion of the grid. It reveals the status of a cell as being free (in gray) or occupied (in black)
  • Remember, the sonar doesn't reveal the location of the ship's captains quarters. You have 2 sonar pulses total in the game and they can be used only after successfully sinking the first enemy ship
